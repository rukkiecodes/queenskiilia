## Full description (max 4000 characters)

```
QueenSkiilia is a marketplace that connects students with verified businesses for paid project work — design, writing, research, web, marketing, video, and more.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Build a verified profile. Add your skills, bio, university, and portfolio. Pass identity verification to earn the Verified badge.

• Find real projects. Browse open projects from businesses around the world. Filter by skill, level, budget, and deadline. Apply with a personal cover note.

• Get paid through escrow. When a business selects you, they fund the project upfront — your earnings sit safely in escrow until the work is approved. No chasing invoices, no surprises.

• Showcase your wins. Approved submissions land in your public portfolio automatically, so future clients can see your track record.

• Chat with your client. Built-in messaging keeps every project conversation in one place. Share files, ask questions, send revisions.

• Settle disputes fairly. If something goes wrong, either side can raise a dispute and our team reviews the evidence before any funds move.

WHO IT'S FOR

QueenSkiilia is built for college and university students who want paid work that fits their schedule, and for businesses (especially small businesses and startups) that want vetted creative talent without recruiter fees.

WHY QUEENSKIILIA

• Verified identity for both sides — no anonymous accounts.
• Escrow on every project — no "the client ghosted me" stories.
• Project-gated ratings — only people who worked together can review each other.
• Transparent fees — a single 10–15% platform commission, disclosed before you confirm a deposit.
• Real moderation — every reported user or project is reviewed by a human within 48 hours.

PRIVACY & CONTROL

You're in charge of your data. Mute any notification category from Settings. Delete your account in one tap, with a 30-day reversibility window. Read our full Privacy Policy and Terms of Service from inside the app.

QueenSkiilia is not a bank. Escrow services are facilitated by Paystack. The platform takes a 10–15% commission on each project. See the in-app Terms for the full refund and dispute policy.

QueenSkiilia uses your camera for identity verification only — ID and face photos are never shared with other users. Push notifications, file uploads, and avatars are all optional.

GETTING STARTED

1. Install QueenSkiilia.
2. Sign in with your email — we'll send a one-time passcode, no password to remember.
3. Pick whether you're a student or a business.
4. Build your profile.
5. Apply to your first project (students) or post your first one (businesses).

If you run into trouble, email support@queenskilla.app — we reply within one business day.

This app is rated Teen (13+). Users under 18 can use the platform with parental consent but can't receive payouts.
```
