## 5. Projects and payment

### Project lifecycle

1. A business posts a project with a budget, currency, deadline, and
   required skills.
2. Students apply. The business reviews and selects one student.
3. Once selected, the business funds escrow: project budget + a platform
   fee (see §6).
4. The student delivers work via the in-app submission flow. The business
   reviews and either approves (releasing the escrow to the student) or
   requests a revision.
5. Either party may raise a dispute at any time after the project is
   funded; the escrow is frozen until the dispute is resolved.

### Escrow and refunds

- **QueenSkiilia is not a bank.** Funds held during a project are held by
  our payment processor (Paystack) on QueenSkiilia's behalf.
- Funds are released **only** on business approval of the submission, on
  a mutually agreed dispute resolution, or on a refund-to-business
  resolution.
- Refunds and disputed releases are at the sole discretion of the
  QueenSkiilia operations team based on the evidence submitted by both
  parties.
- Currency conversion and payment fees charged by Paystack or the
  underlying card network are passed through and are not refundable.

### Cancellation

- A business may cancel a project before a student is selected without
  penalty.
- After selection but before funding, either party may walk away without
  penalty.
- After funding, cancellation requires the dispute flow.
