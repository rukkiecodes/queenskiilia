QueenSkiilia: Global Experience Marketplace Blueprint
1. Brand Identity
- Name: QueenSkiilia
- Tagline: “From Skill to Real Experience”
- Colors: Dark Royal Blue, Gold, Black, White
- UI Style: Modern, premium, fintech-like feel
2. Mission
Connect students, learners, and freelancers with real-world projects to gain verified experience before employment.
3. Target Users
- Students & Learners: university students, online learners, bootcamp graduates, self-taught freelancers

- Businesses & Entrepreneurs: startups, small businesses, agencies, companies scouting talent
4. User Registration & Verification
Account Types:
- Student Account
- Business Account
Verification Steps:
Students:
1. Email verification
2. Phone verification (SMS code)
3. ID verification (passport, national ID, or driver’s license)
4. Face verification (match ID)
Businesses:
1. Email verification
2. Phone verification
3. Business verification: company name, website, country, industry
4. Business proof: registration document, LinkedIn page, or official website
Result: Verified Badge displayed ✔
5. Login & Security
- Two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Captcha or human verification
- AI fraud detection: detects fake accounts, spam, stolen content
- Encrypted user data
- Secure escrow payments
6. Global Features
- Multi-language support (English, Spanish, French, Portuguese initially)
- Multi-currency payments: USD, GBP, EUR, NGN, etc.
- Currency auto-conversion
- Payment gateways: Stripe, PayPal, Flutterwave, Paystack
- Escrow system ensures secure transactions
7. Dashboard Overview
Students:
- Skill level
- Projects available
- Submitted work
- Earnings
- Ratings
- Portfolio

Businesses:
- Posted projects
- Applicants
- Payments
- Top talent suggestions
- Talent leaderboard
8. Skills Assessment
- AI-powered skill test
- Categorizes students as Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert
- Used to match projects
9. Freelance Skills Marketplace
Technology: Software dev, Web dev, Mobile apps, AI/ML, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Cloud, DevOps, Data engineering.

Design: Graphic, UI/UX, Animation, Motion graphics, 3D, Illustration, Branding
Marketing: Social media, SEO, Paid ads, Email marketing, Influencer & affiliate marketing.

Content Creation: Blogging, Copywriting, Scriptwriting, Ghostwriting, Technical writing, Podcast editing.

Video/Media: Video editing, YouTube editing, Cinematography, Sound design, Voice-over.

Data & Analytics: Data analysis, Business intelligence, Financial modeling, Statistics
Business Services: Virtual assistance, Project management, Research, Customer support, Bookkeeping.

Trending Skills: AI prompt engineering, AI automation, No-code development, Web3, AR/VR design, Game dev
10. Project Marketplace
- Businesses post projects with title, budget, skill requirement, and deadline
- Students apply and submit work
- Escrow payment held until approval
- Smart deadlines with reminders & countdown timers
11. Ratings & Reputation System
Students:
- Rated by businesses on quality, communication, speed, professionalism

Businesses:
- Rated by students on payment fairness, clarity, communication, respect

Leaderboards:
- Top talent rankings globally
- Category-based leaderboards
12. Portfolio Builder
- Auto-generates verified portfolio from completed projects
- Includes project descriptions, client reviews, and ratings
13. Escrow & Payment Flow
1. Business deposits payment before project starts
2. Student completes work
3. Business approves → funds released
4. Disputes handled by platform admin
5. Platform takes 10–15% commission
Optional Milestones for larger projects: staged payments (e.g., 30%, 40%, 30%)
14. Dispute Resolution
- Admin reviews project instructions, submission, communication
- Decision made fairly
- Ensures trust between students and businesses
15. Anti-Fraud & Security
- AI fraud detection for plagiarism, copied work, fake projects, bots
- Human verification via Captcha and ID
- Account flagging system
- Escrow payment to prevent non-payment
16. Terms & Conditions
Businesses:
- Must deposit money upfront
- Provide clear instructions
- Respect deadlines
- Avoid harassment

Students:
- Submit original work
- Respect deadlines
- Communicate professionally
- Avoid plagiarism

Platform:
- Prohibits fraud, fake accounts, stolen content, harassment
- Violations lead to account suspension
17. Global Vision
- Launch as global experience marketplace
- Scale to millions of users worldwide
- Solve key complaints: lack of real experience, unsafe payments, unverified clients, unreliable work
18. Screens & Layout
Core Screens:
1. Login/Signup – account type selection, verification
2. Dashboard – overview of projects, progress, earnings, ratings
3. Project Marketplace – browse, filter, apply, post projects
4. Skill Test – AI assessment
5. Submission Screen – upload work, chat with client
6. Portfolio – auto-generated from approved projects
7. Ratings & Reviews – rate and view ratings
8. Payments – deposit, escrow, release funds, currency conversion
9. Leaderboards – global top talent
10. Settings – language, notifications, profile
11. Support/Dispute – raise issues, admin support
12. Notifications – reminders, deadlines, project updates
19. Additional Features
- Language selection
- Badges for Verified Talent and Verified Business
- AI mentor suggestions (for students to improve)
- Alerts for deadlines and project updates
- Global currency support with auto-conversion
- Feedback loop to constantly improve platform

Upwork, and other apps like it


•Freelance marketplace.


•Students or beginners can technically work, but most clients prefer experienced freelancers.


•No guided “learning + skill verification” system.


•Portfolios are manually uploaded, your work is only visible if you convince a client.


•You have to fight for clients, often with low pay if you’re new.




QUEENSKILLIA 


•Learning + working + portfolio at the same time.


•Students don’t just apply randomly, they get projects that match their skill level (AI skill test guides this).


•Work is verified automatically and added to their portfolio.


•Students start building real, proof-of-skill portfolios before they graduate.


•Businesses can hire directly from the platform or scout talent, and the student’s portfolio proves capability.


•Escrow + smart deadlines protect both students and businesses, so there’s less risk.
